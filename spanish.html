Express Yourself Wall

Emojis

This is the project I did in Spanish to try to spread happiness around the school how the people spread happiness around Costa RIca. Me and my peers made an express yourself wall so students, teachers, and staff can write about how they are feeling throughout the day or week. We had used a white board, dry erase markers, emojis, and came up with rules that you can't use any profanity but if so you have to change the words with symbols.
